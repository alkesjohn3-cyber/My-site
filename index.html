Grow Closer to God

By John Alkes

Core Catholic Beliefs

God created you on purpose and loves you deeply. You are never alone.

Jesus Christ is the Son of God who saved us through His death and resurrection.

The Church helps guide us and bring us closer to God through truth and sacraments.

Prayer

Prayer is talking to God honestly.

  • Thank Him
  • Ask for help
  • Listen quietly

How to Focus in Prayer

  • Find a quiet place
  • Put distractions away
  • Start small
  • Bring your mind back

Even distracted prayer still counts.

Going to Mass Consistently

  • Pick a regular time
  • Treat it like a priority
  • Go with others
  • You are meeting Jesus
